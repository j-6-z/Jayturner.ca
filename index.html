About Me

I didn’t take the traditional path — and that’s exactly why my code works in the real world.

My journey kicked off at the beginning of 2024 with The Odin Project — a free, open-source curriculum that drilled the fundamentals of web development into me the hard way: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, and the command line. No hand-holding. Just code, debug, repeat.

From there, I powered through and finished an intensive coding bootcamp in August 2024, where I built production-grade apps from the ground up — no fluff, just shipping real features like CRUD APIs and responsive single-page applications.

In January 2025, I enrolled in Computer Science at Athabasca University (distance learning). But I never went back — by September, I was all-in on hunting for a real job, channeling my skills into practical, impactful work.

Today, I write clean, fast, maintainable code — whether it’s Angular dashboards scaling to thousands of users, Ruby on Rails backends handling heavy traffic, or React apps that load lightning-quick.

I don’t chase trends. I solve problems. And I ship.
