About Me

I didn’t take the traditional path — and that’s exactly why my code works in the real world.

In 2022, I completed an intensive full-stack bootcamp at ROBOGARDEN, where I built production-grade apps from day one — no theory, just shipping. From CRUD APIs to responsive SPAs, I learned by doing.

I also studied Computer Science at Athabasca University (distance learning). I didn’t finish the degree — I was too busy building real systems for real clients. The coursework sharpened my logic, algorithms, and systems thinking.

Before that, I went all-in on The Odin Project — a free, open-source curriculum that taught me the fundamentals of web development the hard way: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, and the command line. No hand-holding. Just code, debug, repeat.

Today, I write clean, fast, maintainable code — whether it’s Angular dashboards used by thousands, Ruby on Rails backends handling millions of requests, or React apps that load in under a second.

I don’t chase trends. I solve problems. And I ship.
